📚 Getting Started

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of Credlink:

  • Complete Your Profile: Add more details to increase your borrowing limit
  • Verify Your Identity: Upload documents for KYC verification
  • Add Funding Source: Link a bank account for quick deposits
  • Explore Lending Opportunities: Browse available loans and choose ones that match your criteria
  • Set Up Automatic Investments: Schedule recurring investments in loans

🔒 Security Tips

Keep your account secure by following these best practices:

  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Use a strong, unique password (8+ characters with mix of characters)
  • Enable two-factor authentication in your settings
  • Review your account activity regularly
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately
